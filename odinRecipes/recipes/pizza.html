Ingredients

Steps

  1. Take wrapping off pizza base
  2. Put base on counter
  3. Open topping containers
  4. Put toppings on pizza
  5. Put pizza on tray then put in oven
  6. Turn oven heat to 200 degrees and leave for 20 mins
  7. Remove pizza once 20 minutes has passed
